Mentorship program

6 January - 30 June 2023| Online

Riga TechGirls mentorship program “TechMentor” gathers professionals in the field of IT and tech with the goal of providing support to women developing their talents, taking their first steps in the IT field or advancing their career further in this field.

When is the deadline for applications?

You can apply until November 12th, 2022

What does the program provide?

Support from a local or an international mentor, opportunity to network and form working groups with other participants of the program, Environment where tp grow, advance and broaden your skills and knowledge

What is the selection sriteria?

We evaluate your motivation and goals. Th assessment will be xarried out by a special working group together with mentors

What is timeline?

Program starts on 6th of January and ends on 30th of June,2023

What competences can I train in TechMentor program?

We have mentors representing 12 tracks:
  • Frontend Development
  • Backend development
  • UX/UI
  • QA
  • Engineering
  • IT support/DevOps
  • Project management
  • Product management
  • Cyber security
  • Data analytics/data science
  • Startups
  • "Step up your game"

What is "Step up your game" mentorship?

It is for ladies with experience that want to “step up their game” in the IT field: boost their skillset, while knowing the basics already: likely finished our previous tracks, have done some work in the IT field or professionals that want to move to the IT field from a non-IT related position.
